Welcome to Urban Heavens


At Urban Heavens we pride ourselves on being your one-stop destination for all your grocery needs. Established in 2002, we have been serving the community with top-quality products and exceptional service for more than a decade

Our History

Founded with a vision to provide the local community with a convenient shopping experience, Urban Heavens started as a humble corner store. Today, we have grown into a well-established supermarket chain with multiple branches across Sri Lanka.


Committed to quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction, we offer a diverse range of products including fresh produce, groceries, household essentials, and specialty items. Our dedication to excellence has earned us the trust and loyalty of our customers, making us a beloved part of the community.

Our Mission

Our mission at Urban Heavens is to provide our customers with the highest quality products, exceptional service, and an enjoyable shopping experience. We are committed to offering a diverse selection of goods at competitive prices while maintaining a welcoming and inclusive environment for all.


Join us on our journey as we continue to grow and serve the community with excellence.

Our Branches


FreshFare Market

  • FreshFare Market prides itself on offering the freshest produce sourced directly from local farms. With a wide selection of organic fruits and vegetables, as well as gourmet pantry items, it's your one-stop shop for quality ingredients.

  • Location: 123 Galle Road, Colombo 03, Sri Lanka

GreenGrocer's Haven

  • GreenGrocer's Haven is a cozy neighborhood market dedicated to promoting sustainable living. From farm-fresh produce to eco-friendly household products, we strive to support local growers and reduce our environmental footprint.

  • Location: 456 Kandy Road, Kandy, Sri Lanka

Orchard Oasis

  • Step into Orchard Oasis and immerse yourself in a paradise of premium produce. From hand-picked apples to luscious berries, our orchard-inspired market offers a bountiful array of nature's finest offerings.

  • Location: 101 Negombo Road, Negombo, Sri Lanka
