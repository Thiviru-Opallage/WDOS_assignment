Our Trusted Vegetable Providers



Green Valey Farms


Location: Kandy

Green Valley Farms is a family-owned organic farm that has been operating for over 20 years. Known for its sustainable farming practices, the farm grows a wide variety of vegetables, including leafy greens, root vegetables, and seasonal specialties. The farm prides itself on using no synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, ensuring that all produce is fresh, healthy, and environment friendly. Customers can visit the farm to see where their food is grown and even participate in seasonal harvesting events.

Urban Greens Cooperative


Location: Matale

Urban Greens Cooperative is an innovative urban farming initiative that focuses on hydroponic and vertical farming methods to grow fresh vegetables in the city. The cooperative consists of multiple small-scale urban farms located on rooftops and in community gardens. They supply a range of vegetables, including tomatoes, peppers, herbs, and microgreens, which are harvested daily to ensure maximum freshness. The cooperative emphasizes local production, reducing the carbon footprint associated with food transportation, and promoting community involvement in sustainable agriculture.

Mountain Side Market Gardens


Location: Nuwara-Eliya

Riverside Market Gardens is a picturesque farm located by the river, benefiting from fertile soil and a favorable microclimate for growing a diverse array of vegetables. The farm produces everything from common vegetables like carrots, potatoes, and onions to more exotic varieties such as heirloom tomatoes and specialty squash. Riverside Market Gardens uses a combination of traditional farming techniques and modern innovations to maximize yield and maintain soil health. The farm is also a popular destination for weekend farmers' markets and educational tours, where visitors can learn about sustainable agriculture and purchase fresh, farm-grown produce directly.
